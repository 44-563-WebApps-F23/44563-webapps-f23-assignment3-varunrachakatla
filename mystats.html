Chicken Biryani

I love this food because Chicken biryani tastes exceptionally good due to the combination of tender, succulent chicken pieces and fragrant, long-grain basmati rice, all infused with a medley of aromatic spices and herbs. The chicken, marinated in yogurt and a blend of spices, absorbs the flavors and becomes incredibly flavorful and moist when cooked. The rice is parboiled and then layered with the marinated chicken, caramelized onions, saffron-infused milk, and aromatic spices before being slow-cooked to perfection
